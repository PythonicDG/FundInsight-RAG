# Small Cap Alpha Fund - Factsheet (March 2024)

## Fund Objective
The Small Cap Alpha Fund seeks to generate long-term capital appreciation by investing predominantly in equity and equity-related instruments of small-cap companies. The fund focuses on high-growth potential businesses with strong management and competitive advantages.

## Fund Details
- **AUM:** $1.2 Billion
- **Inception Date:** January 15, 2018
- **Benchmark:** Nifty Smallcap 250 Index
- **Fund Manager:** Sarah Jenkins

## Sector Allocation
The fund maintains a diversified portfolio across various sectors to mitigate risks while capturing growth. As of March 31, 2024, the top sector allocations are as follows:
The Information Technology sector remains the largest holding at 22.5%, driven by mid-sized software service providers. 
The Capital Goods sector follows closely at 18.2%, reflecting the fund's positive outlook on the manufacturing rebound. 
Consumer Discretionary accounts for 15.8% of the fund, particularly focusing on emerging lifestyle brands. 
Healthcare and Pharmaceuticals represent 12.1% of the portfolio, while Financial Services make up 10.4%, primarily through niche lending institutions.
